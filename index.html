Efficient management of the vessel's inventory

This platform is designed to streamline inventory management on board vessels. Originally tailored for the fishing fleet, the solution offers flexible features suitable for all vessels with large amounts of equipment on board.

Functions and use

The platform allows you to easily register, track, and update equipment, supplies, tools, and safety items. You can quickly generate reports, export to PDF or Excel, and share them with other users or vessels.

With various user roles, structured access is ensured: For example, officers have full control over the inventory, while crew members can register consumption and updates via the Seavo Nautiq Mobile app.

Additional modules

The system also includes modules for travel planning, order tracking, fleet overview, and integration with safety and compliance procedures.

Critical expiry dates – such as for medications, rescue equipment, and batteries – are automatically monitored to ensure compliance with regulations.

The solution works both online and offline, making it reliable even with limited internet coverage at sea.

Getting started

If you are a new user, we recommend starting with the tabs "Office" or "Equipment" to familiarize yourself with how the categories are structured.

You can also find useful resources and guides in the help section.
