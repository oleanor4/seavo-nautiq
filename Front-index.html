Welcome to the Seavo Nautiq platform!

This is a platform for managing vessel inventory efficiently.

Although the platform was initially intended as a tailor-made digital solution for the fishing fleet, the inventory management offers

flexible solutions for other types of vessels with extensive quantities of items on board.

The platform allows you to register, track, and update equipment, provisions, tools, and safety items with ease.

Users can quickly generate inventory reports, export them to PDF or Excel, and share them with other users or vessels when needed.

Multiple user roles ensure structured access — for example, officers can manage full inventory control, while crew members can register consumption and updates.

Additional modules include voyage planning, order tracking, fleet overview, and integration with safety and compliance routines.

Expiration dates for critical items like medicine, rescue equipment, and batteries can be monitored to help ensure regulatory compliance.

The system is designed to work both online and offline, making it a reliable tool even when there is limited internet coverage at sea.

If you are new to the platform, we recommend starting with the "Office" or "Equipment" section to familiarize yourself with how categories are structured.
